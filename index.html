The Problem

“$210 billion a year”- Amount spent on unnecessary healthcare services (Institute for Medicine).
Providers are missing key insights that could lead to better treatment and less spending. They simply don’t have the time to parse through all the data hidden on user-unfriendly EHRs.
One huge unaddressed area is GI bleeding. Right now, too many patients w/ symptoms of a GI bleed are directed to uncomfortable overnight hospital stays when they could be more effectively treated as an outpatient.

Our Solution

We’re partnering with EHR companies and leading hospitals to produce a machine learning and data visualization application directly integrated into the EHR. Our number one focus - helping triage very low risk patients with symptoms of GI bleeding. Right now we are working with the best GI bleeding model ever published.
